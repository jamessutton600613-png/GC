### New manuscript (in submission with JHEP reports)

**Thresholded Immune State Transitions and Lag Dynamics in Therapeutic Peptide Vaccination for Chronic Hepatitis B**  
DOI: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18299345  
A state-based statistical re-analysis of therapeutic vaccination data demonstrating that immune responses do **not** evolve smoothly. Instead, responses occur via **discrete state transitions and lag phases**, helping explain responder and non-responder segregation that is obscured by cohort averaging.
