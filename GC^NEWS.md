## GC-arc programme update (January 2026)

Three closely linked works have now been released on Zenodo and added to the
**Germinal Center (GC) community**, marking a major consolidation point for the
GC-arc / GQR research programme.

Together, these papers formalise a transition from descriptive and statistical
models of germinal-centre dynamics to an explicitly **governed accessibility**
framework, in which immune outcomes are constrained by structural invariants
rather than inferred from cohort-averaged optimisation or probability alone.

### New manuscript (in submission with JHEP reports)

**Thresholded Immune State Transitions and Lag Dynamics in Therapeutic Peptide Vaccination for Chronic Hepatitis B**  
DOI: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18299345  
A state-based statistical re-analysis of therapeutic vaccination data demonstrating that immune responses do **not** evolve smoothly. Instead, responses occur via **discrete state transitions and lag phases**, helping explain responder and non-responder segregation that is obscured by cohort averaging.
